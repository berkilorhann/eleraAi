Privacy Policy

Data Collection

We collect the following user data to provide a better experience and ensure the functionality of our application:

  • Email addresses for user authentication and communication purposes.

Data Storage

All collected data, including email addresses, is securely stored using Firebase. Firebase provides industry-standard encryption and security practices to ensure the safety of user data.

Data Sharing

We do not share any user data, including email addresses, with third parties. If this policy changes in the future, users will be notified in advance and given the option to opt-out.

Account Deletion

To request the deletion of your account, please follow the steps below:

  1. Submit an Account Deletion Request:

    To delete your account, please send an email to the following address:

    Email Address: cosmicloopbyte@gmail.com

  2. Email Content:

    In your email, please include the following information:

    • Your Name
    • Your Email Address
    • User ID associated with your account (if applicable)

Data Retention Period

Once your deletion request is processed, your data may be stored for up to 30 days. After this period, all data will be permanently deleted. However, certain data may be retained for longer periods due to legal obligations.

Deleted Data

The following data will be permanently deleted during the account deletion process:

  • User account information
  • Profile data
  • In-app content (messages, posts, etc.)

The account deletion process typically takes up to 5 business days. However, in case of high demand, this time may be extended.
