Account Deletion Policy

This Account Deletion Policy is part of the broader privacy practices for ChatVibe: My AI Friend, effective as of February 21, 2025. It outlines the process for requesting the deletion of your account and any associated data. ChatVibe: My AI Friend requires users to log in using their Google accounts, and this policy explains how to delete your account and the data linked to it. Please review this carefully.

Account Deletion Request

At ChatVibe: My AI Friend, we prioritize user privacy and secure account management. The app requires users to log in via their Google accounts, and all user-generated data (e.g., chat history) is stored locally on your device. However, certain data, such as account linking information and purchase records, may be stored on our servers. This policy details how to request the deletion of your account and associated data.

1. Submit an Account Deletion Request

To delete your account and associated data directly from the app:

  • Step 1: Open ChatVibe: My AI Friend on your device.
  • Step 2: Navigate to the Settings menu.
  • Step 3: Select "Account" or a similar option (depending on the app's UI).
  • Step 4: Click on the "Delete Account" button.
  • Step 5: Follow the prompts to confirm your account deletion request.
  • Step 6: Uninstall the app to remove all locally stored data, including chat history.

Alternatively, if you cannot access the app, you may contact us directly to initiate the deletion process:

  • Email Address: cosmicloopbyte@gmail.com
  • Subject Line: "Account Deletion Request - ChatVibe: My AI Friend"

2. Request Details

When submitting a deletion request via email, please provide the following information to help us identify your account:

  • Account Email: The email address associated with your Google account used to log in to the app.
  • Google Play Order ID (if applicable): If you have made in-app purchases, include the Order ID from your purchase confirmation email or Google Play transaction history.
  • Additional Context (optional): Any other details that may assist us, such as the approximate date of account creation or last login.

3. Deletion Process

  • Local Data Deletion: Uninstalling the app will immediately delete all locally stored data, such as chat history, as we do not retain this information on our servers.
  • Server-Side Data Deletion: Upon receiving your deletion request, we will:
    • Delete your account linking information (e.g., Google ID).
    • Remove any associated purchase records or technical data stored on our servers.
  • Processing Time: Account deletion requests are processed within 5 business days. In rare cases of high demand or technical delays, this may extend to 15 business days. A confirmation email will be sent upon completion.
  • Verification: We may verify your identity using the email associated with your Google account to ensure the request is legitimate.

4. Data Affected by Deletion

  • Locally Stored Data: Chat history and other content stored on your device are deleted upon uninstallation. We do not have access to this data.
  • Account Linking Data: Information linking your Google account to the app will be permanently deleted from our servers.
  • Purchase Records: Any in-app purchase data associated with your account will be removed from our systems.
  • Technical Data: Anonymized technical data (e.g., crash logs, app usage statistics) may be retained for a limited period but will be disassociated from your account.

5. Data Retention Period

  • Local Data: No retention occurs, as all data is stored on your device and deleted upon uninstallation.
  • Server-Side Data: After processing your deletion request, account-related data is permanently deleted. However, certain anonymized data may be retained for up to 30 days for technical or compliance purposes.
  • Legal Obligations: In exceptional cases, purchase records may be retained longer if required by law (e.g., tax regulations in Turkey or Google Play policies). Such data will be anonymized and used solely for compliance purposes.

6. Post-Deletion

  • Once your account is deleted, you will no longer be able to access the app using the same Google account unless you create a new account.
  • Premium features tied to in-app purchases will no longer be accessible, and any associated data will be removed.
  • Reinstalling the app and logging in with a new account will start a fresh session with no prior data retained.

Important Notes

  • Account-Based System: ChatVibe: My AI Friend requires users to log in using their Google accounts. Account deletion involves removing the link between your Google account and the app, as well as any associated data.

  • No Reversibility: Account deletion is permanent. Once deleted, your account and associated data cannot be recovered.
